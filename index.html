A volunteer project built with React, Express.js, Node.js, and MongoDB

Protect Our Elders serves the elderly in the community who feel unsafe while in public for fear of being targets of violent attacks. This platform is open to any organization that wishes to rally volunteers to chaperone elderly when they wish to travel while feeling secure and protected.
